About Me

Hi! I’m I Kadek Adrian Dwi Suwahyu, but you can call me Adrian. I’m a tech enthusiast from Bali, passionate about exploring how innovation and creativity shape the digital world. I create educational content on TikTok, focusing on technology, blockchain, and the future of the internet. I love diving into the world of programming, learning how ideas turn into real solutions through code. Beyond tech, I’m also deeply connected to my roots — the art of Balinese gamelan (tabuh) is something I truly enjoy, as it reminds me that technology and culture can go hand in hand in creating harmony and inspiration.
